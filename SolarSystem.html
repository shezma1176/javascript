The solar system is full of eerie wonders and mind-bending oddities that defy expectations. From planets that rain diamonds to moons that may harbor alien oceans, it’s a cosmic playground of the bizarre.

Saturn’s moon Titan has lakes of liquid methane and ethane, making it one of the most Earth-like yet alien places in the solar system

Uranus spins on its side, likely due to a massive ancient collision, giving it extreme seasons that last over 20 years

Io, a moon of Jupiter, is the most volcanically active body in the solar system, with eruptions that can shoot lava hundreds of kilometers high
